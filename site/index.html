ROUTER

Bring your own keys

fal, Replicate, or a self-hosted box. One catalog, seven models, automatic fallback to whichever key you hold. Zero markup.

PRESETS

61 cinematic presets

Camera moves, lenses, lighting, film stocks, VFX, mood, transitions — stackable, model-agnostic prose. The exact thing behind Higgsfield's paywalled gallery.

CINEMA STUDIO

Real optics

Choose a camera body, lens, focal length, aperture, and shot size. openfield composes it into glass-accurate direction — their flagship, open.

SOUL ID

Character consistency

Save a character once — reference images plus look traits — and lock the same face across every shot and every model.

DIRECTOR

The real Hermes

Runs actual Nous Hermes 3 in a multi-tool function-calling loop — it searches presets, inspects the cinema catalog, and directs your storyboard shot by shot. Same brain as their Hermes, open.
